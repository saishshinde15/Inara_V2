## 1. Leave Policies

### Annual Leave (PTO)
- Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off per year
- PTO accrues monthly at 1.67 days per month
- Maximum carryover: 5 days to next year
- Request leave through the HR portal with at least 2 weeks notice

### Sick Leave
- 10 days of sick leave per year
- Doctor's note required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days
- Unused sick leave does not carry over

### Parental Leave
- Maternity leave: 16 weeks paid
- Paternity leave: 8 weeks paid
- Adoption leave: 12 weeks paid

### Other Leave Types
- Bereavement leave: Up to 5 days
- Jury duty: Full pay during service
- Voting leave: Up to 2 hours

## 2. Benefits Overview

### Health Insurance
- Medical, dental, and vision coverage
- Company covers 80% of premium costs
- Coverage begins on first day of employment
- Open enrollment period: November 1-30

### Retirement Benefits
- 401(k) plan available after 90 days
- Company matches 100% up to 4% of salary
- Additional 50% match on next 2% of salary
- Immediate vesting on company match

### Wellness Program
- $500 annual wellness reimbursement
- Gym membership discounts
- Mental health support through EAP

## 3. Performance Management

### Annual Reviews
- Conducted in Q4 each year
- Self-assessment due by November 1
- Manager feedback provided by December 15
- Compensation adjustments effective January 1

### Goal Setting
- Goals set in Q1 using OKR framework
- Mid-year check-in in Q2
- Quarterly progress discussions

## 4. Code of Conduct

### Professional Behavior
- Treat all colleagues with respect
- Maintain confidentiality of company information
- Report conflicts of interest
- Follow IT security policies

### Dress Code
- Business casual in office
- Client meetings: business professional
- Casual Fridays permitted

## 5. Expense Reimbursement

### Travel Expenses
- Book through corporate travel portal
- Per diem: $75/day for meals
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Business class for international flights over 6 hours

### Expense Reporting
- Submit within 30 days of expense
- Receipts required for expenses over $25
- Use corporate card when available
- Manager approval required for expenses over $500

### Reimbursement Timeline
- Submissions processed weekly
- Reimbursement within 2 pay cycles
- Direct deposit to payroll account
