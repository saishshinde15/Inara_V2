# Master Actions Guide

## HR Actions and Procedures

This document provides step-by-step guidance for common HR actions.

---

## 1. Requesting Time Off

### Steps:
1. Log into the HR Portal at hr.company.com
2. Navigate to "Time Off" â†’ "Request Leave"
3. Select the type of leave (PTO, Sick, etc.)
4. Choose start and end dates
5. Add any notes for your manager
6. Submit request

### Approval Timeline:
- PTO: 3-5 business days
- Sick leave: Same day for urgent requests
- Extended leave: 2 weeks

### Requirements:
- 2 weeks notice for planned PTO
- Doctor's note for sick leave > 3 days

---

## 2. Submitting Expense Reports

### Steps:
1. Log into the Expense Portal
2. Click "New Expense Report"
3. Select expense category
4. Enter amount and date
5. Upload receipt image
6. Add business justification
7. Submit for approval

### Receipt Requirements:
- Required for expenses > $25
- Must show vendor, date, and amount
- Credit card statements not accepted alone

### Approval Chain:
- Under $500: Direct manager
- $500-$2000: Department head
- Over $2000: Finance approval

---

## 3. Enrolling in Benefits

### Open Enrollment (November 1-30):
1. Access benefits portal at benefits.company.com
2. Review current elections
3. Compare plan options
4. Make selections for next year
5. Add/update dependents if needed
6. Confirm elections before deadline

### Life Events:
Qualifying events allow mid-year changes:
- Marriage/divorce
- Birth/adoption
- Loss of other coverage

Submit documentation within 30 days of event.

---

## 4. Performance Review Process

### Self-Assessment (Due November 1):
1. Access performance portal
2. Review goals set at start of year
3. Document achievements with metrics
4. Identify areas for development
5. Submit self-assessment

### Manager Review (November-December):
1. Manager reviews self-assessment
2. Adds their evaluation
3. Assigns performance rating
4. Schedules feedback meeting
5. Discussion and goal-setting for next year

### Rating Scale:
- 5: Exceptional
- 4: Exceeds Expectations
- 3: Meets Expectations
- 2: Needs Improvement
- 1: Unsatisfactory

---

## 5. Onboarding New Employees

### Manager Checklist:
1. Submit hire request to HR
2. Request equipment from IT
3. Prepare workspace
4. Schedule first-week meetings
5. Assign onboarding buddy
6. Create 30/60/90 day plan

### New Employee Day 1:
1. Report to HR for orientation
2. Complete I-9 and tax forms
3. Receive equipment and access
4. Meet with manager
5. Review onboarding schedule

---

## 6. Requesting Equipment

### Standard Equipment:
- Laptop: Automatically provided
- Monitor: Request through IT portal
- Headset: Standard for remote workers

### Special Requests:
1. Submit request through IT portal
2. Include business justification
3. Manager approval required
4. Delivery within 5-7 business days

---

## 7. Updating Personal Information

### What Can Be Updated:
- Address and phone number
- Emergency contacts
- Tax withholding (W-4)
- Direct deposit information
- Benefits beneficiaries

### How to Update:
1. Log into HR Portal
2. Go to "My Profile"
3. Select information to update
4. Make changes
5. Confirm with verification code

### Important Notes:
- Address changes: Update within 30 days of moving
- Tax changes: Take effect next pay period
- Benefits changes: May require supporting documentation
